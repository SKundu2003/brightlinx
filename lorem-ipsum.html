Why Use Placeholder Text?

Using Lorem Ipsum allows designers to focus on visual elements (layout, typography, color) without being distracted by specific content. If you use real text during the design phase, clients or stakeholders often focus on reading the copy rather than critiquing the design itself.

Key benefits:

  • Visual Balance: Provides a natural-looking block of text (unlike repeating "Text here").
  • Efficiency: Quickly fill layouts without waiting for copywriters.
  • Neutrality: Prevents premature feedback on content that isn't final.
