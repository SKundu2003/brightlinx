How does the Small Text Generator work?

Many people think this is a "small font" that you can download, but it is actually a set of Unicode characters. Unicode is the international standard for text on computers, and it includes ranges for phonetic symbols, mathematical subscript/superscript, and older writing systems.

This tool maps your regular keyboard letters (A-Z) to these specific Unicode symbols that happen to look like smaller versions of the alphabet. Because they are standard text characters, you can copy and paste them anywhereâ€”Instagram bios, Facebook posts, Twitter tweets, and more.

Instagram & TikTok Bios: Make your profile stand out with a unique aesthetic that catches the eye.

Gamer Tags: Create a unique username for Discord, PUBG, or Fortnite using superscript letters.

Aesthetic Captions: Use small caps to add a "whisper" effect or a minimalist vibe to your photo captions.

Why do some letters look missing?

You might notice that the "Subscript" generator sometimes replaces letters with similar-looking symbols or leaves them blank. This is because the Unicode standard does not actually include a dedicated subscript character for every single letter of the alphabet.
