How does the Text Repeater work?

Using our text repeater is incredibly simple, just follow these 3 steps:

  1. Type the text or emoji you want to repeat in the "Text to Repeat" box.
  2. Enter the number of times you want to repeat it (up to 10,000 times).
  3. Choose your separator (like a new line or a space) and click "Repeat Text".

Once generated, you can copy it with one click or share it directly to WhatsApp to surprise your friends.

Features of this Tool

Newline Support: Unlike some basic repeaters, we allow you to separate each repetition with a new line, making it perfect for long lists or vertical text art.

Emoji Compatible: Works perfectly with all emojis, special characters, and non-English scripts.

Instant Copy: Our "Copy Text" button ensures you can grab your 10,000 words in a microsecond without selecting them manually.

Why use a Text Repeater?

Text repeaters are popularly used for sending playful messages to friends (often called "text bombing" in a friendly context), creating emphasis in social media posts, or generating filler text for testing designs. Whether you want to say "I love you" 100 times or create a wall of text for a Discord server, this tool handles it instantly.
