How does the Percentage Calculator work?

Percent means "per 100". To calculate a percentage, you are essentially determining a part of a whole expressed as a fraction of 100. The basic formula to find a percentage of a number is:

(Percentage / 100) * Number

For example, to find 20% of 500, you calculate (20 / 100) * 500 = 0.2 * 500 = 100.

Common Percentage Examples

Example Calculation Result
10% of 100 0.10 * 100 10
50% of 80 0.50 * 80 40
25% of 200 0.25 * 200 50

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I calculate a percentage increase?

To calculate percentage increase: ((New Value - Old Value) / Old Value) * 100. If the result is positive, it's an increase.

How do I calculate a percentage decrease?

It's the same formula as increase. If the result is negative, it represents a percentage decrease.

What is X as a percentage of Y?

To find what percent X is of Y, use the formula: (X / Y) * 100.
