What is Dummy Text?

"Dummy text" or "placeholder text" is text that has no meaningful content but looks like real text. It helps designers show how a page will look (the flow, fonts, and layout) without the distraction of meaningful content. The most famous form of dummy text is Lorem Ipsum.

Origins of Lorem Ipsum
