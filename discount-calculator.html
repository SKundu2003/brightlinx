How does the Discount Calculator work?

Calculating a discount is a straightforward process. The basic formula to find the discount amount is to multiply the original price by the discount percentage represented as a decimal. Then, subtract that amount from the original price to get the final sale price.

Formula: Original Price - (Original Price * Discount / 100)

For example, if a shirt costs $50 and is 20% off: $50 * 0.20 = $10 discount. $50 - $10 = $40 final price.
