How does the Love Calculator work?

Our True Love Calculator works by analyzing the compatibility of two names to generate a percentage score. While many love calculators just generate random numbers, our tool uses a special algorithm that assigns a numerical value to each letter in the names.

This means if you enter "Romeo" and "Juliet" today, you will get the same score tomorrow. This deterministic approach allows you to check compatibility with consistency.

Is this accurate?

This tool is designed purely for entertainment and fun. Real relationships rely on trust, communication, and shared values—not just a score generated from your names!

However, it is a fun way to tease your friends, check your "crush compatibility," or just have a laugh. Share your results on social media to see who gets the highest score!
