What is this Tool?

This is a versatile 2-in-1 time management tool including a Stopwatch (counting up) and a Countdown Timer (counting down). Whether you are timing laps, cooking an egg, or doing the Pomodoro technique for study, this simple web utility helps you stay on track.

How to Use the Stopwatch?

  • Step 1: Ensure the "Stopwatch" tab is selected.
  • Step 2: Press the green "Start" button to begin counting up from zero.
  • Step 3: Press "Stop" to pause the time.
  • Step 4: Press "Reset" to clear the time back to 00:00:00.

How to Use the Timer?

  • Step 1: Click the "Timer" tab.
  • Step 2: Enter your desired time in Hours, Minutes, and Seconds (e.g., 00 : 05 : 00 for 5 minutes).
  • Step 3: Press "Start". The clock will count down.
  • Step 4: When it hits zero, it will stop (and in a real app, alarm sound would play).

Why is it useful?

Online stopwatches are great because they are accessible on any device with a browser, no app installation required. Use it for:

  • Fitness: Timing planks, sprints, or rest sets.
  • Productivity: Time-blocking your work tasks.
  • Cooking: Setting reminders for the oven.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it keep running if I switch tabs?

Yes, modern browsers will keep the logic running, though power-saving modes on mobile might affect accuracy over very long periods (hours).
